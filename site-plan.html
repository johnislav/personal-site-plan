My Personal Site Plan

Purpose and Audience

Purpose: This site will showcase my personal interests, hobbies, and provide a platform for me to introduce myself to others. It will also help me practice HTML and CSS while learning web development fundamentals.

Audience: My classmates, friends, and anyone interested in learning more about me in an easy and engaging way.

Logo

For my personal site, I will create a logo using Canva. The logo will reflect my personality and the content of my site, representing both my creative and professional sides.

Colors and Fonts

Color Scheme: I plan to use a modern and clean color scheme with the following colors:

  • Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green)
  • Secondary Color: #FF9800 (Orange)
  • Accent Color: #2196F3 (Blue)

Fonts: I will use 'Roboto' as my primary font, and 'Montserrat' for headings.

Child Pages

I will create at least two child pages: "About Me" and "My Projects". These pages will link back to the home page and contain additional content about my background and work.
